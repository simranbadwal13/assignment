One:1

The hp-to-kg curb weight ratio is an astonishing 1:1. This is the "dream" equation, previously thought impossible when it comes to fully road legal and usable sports cars. The One:1 is the first homologated production car in the world with one Megawatt of power, thereby making it the world´s first series produced Megacar.

HOW TO GET YOUR TICKET. . . . .

Go the Koenigsegg Factory Tours page on our webshop. Here, you can select the date you want to visit and purchase your ticket.
You should be able to download your ticket at the time of purchase. The download link should appear on the receipt screen after you make payment. Do not worry if the download link does not appear - every ticket is emailed to the buyer as a backup.
The ticket contains a unique QR code. Bring the ticket with you when you come to the tour so that you can be checked in. The ticket can be scanned in either paper or electronic format.

NOTES:

1)

All bookings are final and must be paid for at the time of purchase. All payments are made via Paypal.

2)

Factory tours are approximately 90 minutes in length.

3

All Koenigsegg factory tours are guided tours. Guests are not permitted to enter areas of the factory unaccompanied and must follow any instructions given by the guide.

4)

Photography is allowed in the showroom area, however cameras in any form (e.g. phones) are not allowed inside the factory.

5)

Children must be at least 10 years of age to attend this tour and must be accompanied by a responsible adult.
